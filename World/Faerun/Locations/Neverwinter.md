**Neverwinter** stands as one of the most important cities along the **Sword Coast**, a place defined as much by its resilience as by its ambition. Built along the Neverwinter River where it meets the sea, the city has long been a hub of trade, craftsmanship, and culture. Its nickname, the _City of Skilled Hands_, reflects a population known for artisanship and industry, where blacksmiths, masons, and merchants alike contribute to its steady prosperity.

Yet Neverwinter’s history is marked by catastrophe. The eruption of nearby **Mount Hotenow** brought devastation to the city, reducing much of it to ruin and leaving scars that are still visible today. Entire districts were lost, and what remains is a city in the midst of rebuilding—partially restored grandeur standing beside shattered remnants of the past. This ongoing recovery has created both opportunity and tension, as wealth, power, and influence are constantly in flux.

The city is currently ruled by **Dagult Neverember**, a determined and calculating leader focused on restoring Neverwinter to its former glory. Under his rule, order has returned, but not without controversy. His methods, while effective, have earned both loyalty and resentment among the city’s factions, nobles, and common folk.

For adventurers, Neverwinter is a city of beginnings. Its streets are filled with opportunity, from mercenary work and political intrigue to expeditions into nearby ruins and wild lands. Beneath its rebuilding façade lies a place still haunted by its past, where danger and discovery are never far from reach.

The party started their campaign here.
