**Wave Echo Cave** lies hidden within the rugged hills near Phandalin, its entrance long lost to time and memory. Once, it was the heart of a thriving mine and the site of great magical industry, where dwarves and gnomes worked in secret alongside human spellcasters to harness a rare and powerful phenomenon deep within the earth.

At the center of the cave is the legendary Forge of Spells, an ancient magical source that imbued weapons and armor with enchantments beyond the skill of ordinary craftsmen. The cave itself is named for the strange resonance that echoes through its chambers—a low, rhythmic hum that carries along the stone like a distant heartbeat, a lingering sign of the magic that once flowed freely within.

Centuries ago, this prosperity came to a violent end. The mine was overrun during a brutal conflict as rival forces sought control of the forge, leaving its halls filled with death and ruin. In the aftermath, the location of Wave Echo Cave was lost, its legacy fading into rumor and half-remembered legend.

Now, the cave stands as a shadow of what it once was. Its passages are unstable, its chambers haunted by the remnants of its fall, and its magic diminished but not extinguished. Yet the promise of the Forge of Spells endures, drawing treasure seekers, scholars, and opportunists alike—each hoping to reclaim the power buried within its depths, and each risking becoming another echo in the stone.
