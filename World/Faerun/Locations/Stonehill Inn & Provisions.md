# Stonehill Inn & Provisions

## Description
Stonehill Inn and Barthen's Provisions is a spacious building made of sturdy wood, with a covered porch that invites weary travelers to rest. Inside, the inn features a warm and rustic atmosphere, with wooden beams overhead and walls adorned with various memorabilia from the region’s history. The main room is filled with mismatched tables and chairs, where locals gather to share stories over mugs of ale or hearty meals. A large fireplace crackles in one corner, casting a welcoming glow and adding to the charm of the place.

The shop section, Barthen's Provisions, is filled with shelves stocked with supplies, food, and essentials for both locals and travelers. Bart has a keen eye for quality goods, ensuring his shop is well-stocked with everything from dried meats and grains to simple tools and equipment. He often engages in friendly banter with customers, providing recommendations and sharing local gossip.

Overall, Bart serves as a cornerstone of the community, a trusted figure in Phandalin who embodies the spirit of the town—a blend of strength, resilience, and camaraderie.
