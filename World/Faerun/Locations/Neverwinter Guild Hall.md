# Neverwinter Guild Hall

## Description
The Guild Trading Hall in Neverwinter is a grand, sprawling structure resembling a massive indoor marketplace, alive with the hustle and bustle of traders, artisans, and customers. The hall is an architectural marvel, built from polished stone and dark wood beams, with vaulted ceilings adorned with banners representing various guilds. Skylights high above let in shafts of sunlight that spill onto the polished stone floor, reflecting off the goods displayed at the many stalls. The air is filled with the mingled scents of wood shavings, freshly tanned leather, and the rich aroma of spiced meats from nearby food vendors.

Each stall is uniquely decorated, reflecting the nature of the guild or artisan within. Some are lined with shelves displaying finely crafted tools or shimmering potions, while others showcase intricate weapons, enchanted trinkets, or exotic fabrics. Lanterns, banners, and tapestries hang above the stalls, denoting each guild’s crest, adding a splash of color to the otherwise utilitarian space. The sound of bartering, clanging metal, and soft incantations fills the air, creating a constant hum of activity.

### The Stalls:

1. **The Hammer and Anvil**: A smithy’s stall lined with racks of glistening weapons—swords, axes, and shields—all gleaming under the light of enchanted lanterns. The blacksmith, a burly dwarf with soot-streaked arms, hammers away at an anvil, crafting a new blade while offering pre-made items for inspection.
    
2. **Arcane Wonders**: A mystical booth draped in dark purple fabrics, filled with shelves of glowing potions, wands, and enchanted items. Crystal balls float lazily in the air, and arcane runes shimmer on scrolls laid out for sale. The stall is managed by a thin, elderly half-elf who mutters incantations under his breath as he attends to curious customers.
    
3. **Woodland Wares**: A stall run by a halfling ranger, displaying handcrafted bows, arrows fletched with colorful feathers, and finely polished wooden figurines. The scent of pine and fresh leaves wafts from bundles of herbs and flowers for sale, gathered from deep within the Neverwinter Wood.
    
4. **Innovations** *By Siv*: A mechanical marvel of a stall, cluttered with strange gadgets, clockwork devices, and prototypes of magical inventions. A gnome artificer enthusiastically demonstrates a mechanical bird to an intrigued crowd, while blueprints for custom inventions are pinned on the walls behind him.
    
5. **The Golden Thread**: A weaver’s stall draped with fine silks, linens, and exotic fabrics, all dyed in vibrant hues. A delicate loom sits at the back, with the weaver—a gnome—working swiftly to produce detailed tapestries. Silver and gold thread glimmers in the light as rolls of cloth are neatly arranged for buyers to browse.
    
6. **Gemcutter’s Delight**: A glittering display of raw gemstones, polished jewels, and delicate jewelry pieces. The stall owner, a meticulous dwarf, polishes a ruby as he shows off his more extravagant works, including rings, necklaces, and bracelets studded with rare gems.
    
7. **The Sizzling Spit**: A food stall run by a half-orc chef offering grilled meats, spiced stews, and roasted vegetables to hungry patrons. The scent of sizzling venison and freshly baked bread wafts through the air, drawing in a steady stream of adventurers eager for a hearty meal.
