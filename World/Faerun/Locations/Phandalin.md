## History
More than 500 years ago, clans of Dwarves and Gnomes made an agreement known as the Phandelver Pact, by which that would share a rich mine in a wonderous cavern known as [[Wave Echo Cave]]. In addition to its mineral wealth, the mine contained great magical power. Human spellcasters allied themselves with dwarves and gnomes to channel and bind that energy into a great forge called the [[Forge of Spells]], where magical items could be crafted.

Times were good, and the nearby town of Phandelin prospered as well. But disaster struck when the town was raided by bandits that swept through the North and laid waste to all in their path.

A powerful bandit force reinforced by evil mercenary wizards attacked the [[Wave Echo Cave]] to seize its riches and magical treasures. Human wizards fought alongside their dwarf and gnome allies to defend the [[Forge of Spells]], and the ensuing spell battle destroyed much of the cavern. Few survived the cave-ins and tremors, and knowledge of the location of the cave  was lost to time. 

For centuries, rumors of buried riches have attracted treasure seekers and opportunists to the areas around Phandalin, but no one has located the lost mine. In recent years, people resettled the area, and Phandalin is now a rough-and-tumble frontier town.

## Introductory Description
- Emerging from the dense trees atop a hill, you gaze down upon the frontier town of Phandalin, bathed in the warm glow of the setting sun. Nestled in a lush valley, the modest settlement boasts weathered wooden buildings lining its main street, with the inviting [[Stonehill Inn & Provisions]] standing out as lively hubs of activity. The soft sound of laughter drifts from the inn, mingling with the gentle trickle of a nearby creek where children play. Framed by fields and dotted with farmsteads, the town hums with the simple joys of daily life; townsfolk exchange greetings and gossip, while a farmer guides his oxen toward the well. With its rugged charm and vibrant community, Phandalin beckons you closer, promising adventure and secrets waiting to be uncovered in its heart.
