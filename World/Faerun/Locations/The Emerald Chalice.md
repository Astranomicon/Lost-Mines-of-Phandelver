# The Emerald Chalice

## Description
The **Emerald Chalice** is a cozy, bustling tavern nestled in the heart of [[Neverwinter]], with a warm and inviting atmosphere. Its name comes from the ornate, glowing emerald chalice displayed proudly behind the bar—said to be a relic of an old age, gifted to the tavern by an adventurer long ago. The tavern is bathed in soft amber light from a series of lanterns hanging from the dark wooden beams overhead, casting a warm glow across the stone floors and weathered oak tables.

The walls are adorned with faded tapestries depicting scenes of distant lands and heroic battles, adding a touch of old-world charm. In one corner, a large fireplace crackles, filling the air with the comforting scent of burning wood. Above it, a mounted wyvern head serves as a reminder that even the most welcoming of places have a history of danger and adventure.

The bar is manned by a jovial halfling with ruddy cheeks, busy polishing mugs as he trades stories and jokes with the patrons. The tables are filled with a diverse mix of travelers, adventurers, and merchants, their quiet conversations and occasional laughter creating a low hum of activity. The room is spacious but retains an intimate feeling, with alcoves and corners providing just enough privacy for quiet discussions—or secret deals.

You all enter the building and spot Gundren Rockseeker near the bar. His eyes catch you all entering and gestures the party toward a secluded table in the corner, away from prying eyes, the smell of roasting meat and fresh-baked bread wafts through the air. The clinking of tankards and the murmur of conversation provide a perfect backdrop for what is about to be an important proposition.
