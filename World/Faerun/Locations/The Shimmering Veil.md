# The Shimmering Veil

### **Shrine Name: The Shimmering Veil**

**Description:**  
The Shimmering Veil is a modest yet inviting shrine nestled in a quiet corner of Phandalin, adorned with delicate silver accents that catch the light, giving it an ethereal glow. The building itself is constructed from local stone, with tall, narrow windows that allow soft, filtered light to illuminate the interior. The windows are stained with vibrant hues of purple and blue, depicting scenes of celebration and the moonlit dances of Eilistraee's followers.

Inside, the shrine features an open space for gatherings and rituals, with a polished wooden altar at the center draped in silken fabrics. Around the altar, small altars dedicated to various aspects of Eilistraee line the walls, each adorned with offerings of flowers, silver coins, and personal tokens from the community. The air is filled with the subtle fragrance of incense and blooming nightflowers, creating a serene atmosphere for worship and reflection. 

This shrine serves as a place for guidance, healing, and community gatherings, where followers of Eilistraee come together to honor life, death, and the dance between the two in the spirit of love and unity.

### **Home Description: The Shimmering Veil**

Cithrel’s home, seamlessly attached to The Shimmering Veil shrine, embodies a blend of warmth and tranquility, reflecting the family’s devotion and lifestyle. The structure features rustic wooden beams and walls adorned with tapestries depicting the teachings of Eilistraee, creating a sense of continuity between their sacred space and personal living area.

As you enter through a decorative archway from the shrine into the home, you’re greeted by a cozy foyer that leads into an open-concept living area. This space is bathed in soft, natural light, filtered through the tall windows shared with the shrine. Comfortable furnishings, including plush couches and a large, well-worn wooden table, invite gatherings and discussions. The table is often adorned with fresh flowers and offerings from the community, blending the sacred and the everyday.

The kitchen, open to the living area, is filled with the aroma of herbs and spices. Shelves lined with jars of dried ingredients and freshly baked goods reflect Kialin’s skills as a healer and cook. A small hearth crackles warmly in the corner, providing both heat and a gathering spot for family and visitors.

Leading off the living area are private quarters for Cithrel's parents, simple yet lovingly decorated with personal mementos and items reflecting their life’s work. Cithrel's room is filled with books on spirituality and healing, alongside her own keepsakes, including the dark hand-made band her mother gifted her.

The home and shrine together create a harmonious environment where the Torghasta family lives, worships, and serves the community, embodying the principles of life, death, and the interconnectedness of all things.
