# Bart

## Description
**Race:** Half-Orc
**Class/Role:** Fighter/Pugelist
**Appearance:** Bart is a tall, muscular half-orc with broad shoulders and a commanding presence, standing out among the townsfolk of Phandalin. His skin has a greenish hue, and his facial features are rugged, accentuated by a strong jawline and prominent tusks that jut slightly from his lower lip. Bart’s hair is dark and long, often tied back in a ponytail, and he sports a thick beard that’s well-groomed, framing his face and adding to his imposing appearance. He typically wears a simple yet sturdy outfit of a loose-fitting shirt and durable trousers, often topped with an apron when working behind the bar.

**Personality:**  
Bart is known for his gruff exterior but has a heart of gold beneath his tough demeanor. He’s a hard worker, often seen bustling about the inn, serving patrons and ensuring everything runs smoothly. His booming laughter can often be heard echoing through the building, bringing a sense of warmth and camaraderie to the establishment. Bart is also quite protective of his customers and the town, making him a natural leader among the locals. Despite his intimidating appearance, he’s quick to lend a helping hand and offers wise advice to those who seek it.
