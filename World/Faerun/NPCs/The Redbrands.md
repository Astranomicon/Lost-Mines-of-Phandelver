# The Redbrands

## Description
**Race:** Mixed
**Class/Role:** Bandits
**Appearance:** Variety of individuals all wearing red cloaks and red masks

#### **Purpose:**
	- Antagonize the players
	- Surpress and exploit the people

#### **Roleplaying:**
	- Players either will meet the Redbrands out and about on the streets when they get jumped
	- Or, the players head to the Sleeping Giant Taphouse
	- Play them like old-timey mafia gangsters

#### **What do they know?**
	- Local militia with good motives until a wizard named Glass-staff joined and the local folks got fewer.
		- The rebranded group seized control of the town and the people there walk in fear more than they don't.
	- They know the location of the secret entrances to the hideout
		- They know little about the nothic, as they are mostly afraid of it.
	- They know the sherriff is in the prison.
