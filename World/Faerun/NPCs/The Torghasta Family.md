# The Torghasta Family

### **Family Overview**

**Father: Nyloth Torghasta**
- **Race:** Drow
- **Class/Role:** War Domain Cleric
- **Role**: High Priest specializing in Cleansings.
- **Appearance**: Nyloth is tall and imposing, with dark skin that has a bluish tint and silver hair tied back in a neat braid. His pale silver eyes are sharp and discerning, often reflecting a deep conviction. He wears ornate robes adorned with symbols of purification and wards against unholy entities.
- **Personality**: Nyloth is a strict and dedicated figure, deeply committed to his role in the community. He instills a profound hatred of unlife in his children, believing that it is a corruption that must be eradicated. While he can be stern and demanding, his intentions are rooted in a desire to protect and serve the living. He has high expectations for Cithrel and Chandra, often pushing them to confront their fears and weaknesses head-on.


**Mother: Kialin Torghasta**
- **Race:** Drow
- **Class/Role:** Life Domain Cleric
- **Role**: High Priestess focused on healing services.
- **Appearance**: Kialin is graceful, with long, flowing dark purple hair and an ethereal glow to her skin. She has a warm presence, often wearing flowing robes of deep violet decorated with healing symbols. Her kind, pale silver eyes exude compassion and wisdom. Her eyes are covered by a band of cloth that is placed around their eyes like a veil.
- **Personality**: Kialin is nurturing and empathetic, embodying the healing aspect of their family’s legacy. She emphasizes the importance of preserving life and the dignity of those who are not yet ready to pass on. While she supports her husband’s beliefs, she often acts as a mediator in their household, reminding her daughters that compassion and understanding are equally important. She gifted Cithrel the dark hand-made band, believing it would enhance her connection to souls.

**Twin Sister: Chandra Torghasta**
- **Race:** Drow
- **Class/Role:** Death Domain Cleric
- **Role**: Rebel against the family legacy.
- **Appearance**: Chandra mirrors Cithrel in stature and skin tone, but her hair is longer and more unkempt, often adorned with colorful accessories that contrast with her family’s austere styles. Her eyes, also pale silver, reflect a rebellious spirit.
- **Personality**: Chandra has always felt the weight of expectations placed upon her by her parents. While she shares Cithrel's understanding of life and death, she struggles to accept the rigidity of their family’s path. Chandra often questions their beliefs about unlife and finds herself drawn to the chaotic and unpredictable aspects of existence. This tension creates friction between her and Cithrel, who embraces their parents' teachings wholeheartedly. Chandra often experiments with alternative practices that explore the boundaries between life and death, much to the dismay of her father.

### **Family Dynamics**
The Torghasta family is steeped in a tradition of service to both the living and the departed. Nyloth’s strict beliefs about unlife often clash with Kialin’s compassionate approach, leading to nuanced discussions about their philosophies. Cithrel embodies the balance between her parents' teachings, while Chandra's rebellion adds tension and drama to their household.

The gift of the dark hand-made band from Kialin marks a significant turning point for Cithrel, reinforcing her commitment to the family legacy while also drawing a line between her path and her sister’s. As Cithrel embraces her role in the family business, she may find herself struggling to reconcile her beliefs with her sister's choices, leading to both conflict and growth as they navigate their paths in a world filled with life and death.
