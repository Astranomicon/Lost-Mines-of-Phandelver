## Intro To Harbin
As you make your way through Phandalin’s modest streets, you eventually arrive at the townmaster’s hall, a simple stone building standing taller than the surrounding structures. Inside, the hall smells faintly of ink and aged wood. Behind a large oak desk, slouched in a well-worn chair, sits **[[Harbin Wester]]**, the townmaster.
- He’s a rotund man, his fine clothes ill-suited to his small-town office. A thick ring of keys dangles lazily from his belt, and a gold chain with an oversized medallion rests over his chest, clearly meant to project authority. His thinning hair is combed over in an unconvincing attempt to hide his balding scalp, and as he spots you entering, his watery eyes dart around nervously before settling on a forced smile.
	- "Ah, yes! Adventurers! You must be here to help with… the... situation." His voice rises a little too eagerly, a hint of bravado poorly masking his anxiety. "Well, I’m sure it’s nothing you can’t handle. Just, um... get it sorted out quickly, will you?"
- Despite his apparent attempts to sound in control, there's no mistaking the beads of sweat forming on his brow. You get the impression that Harbin Wester is a man who much prefers the safety of his desk to the dangers beyond Phandalin's walls.
