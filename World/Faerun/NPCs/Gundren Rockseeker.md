# Gundren Rockseeker

## Description
**Race:** Dwarf
**Class/Role:** Bard
**Appearance:** Gundren Rockseeker is a sturdy, broad-shouldered dwarf with a thick, well-groomed beard that reaches his chest, streaked with silver and auburn. His skin is weathered and tanned from years of hard labor in mines and on the road, with deep-set, intelligent eyes that glint with both determination and warmth. His bushy eyebrows often rise in excitement when discussing his next big project, and his smile is wide and infectious, showing a few gold-capped teeth. He’s dressed in well-worn traveling leathers, reinforced with metal studs, and carries a satchel over one shoulder, bulging with maps and tools. His stout frame and confident posture show that, despite his gruff exterior, he’s always ready for the next adventure.
