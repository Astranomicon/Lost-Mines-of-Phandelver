**Name:** Desire
**Race:** Tiefling  
**Occupation:** Owner & bartender of the Sleeping Giant Tavern, Phandalin  

---

# Backstory

## Before Phandalin

Desire spent most of their life on the Triboar Trail as a caravan cook and drink-maker. They learned quickly that a tiefling survives not by winning people over — but by becoming _useful_.

They remembered everyone:

- favorite meals
- griefs
- secrets
- heartbreaks

They became someone people spoke _to_, but never someone people truly knew. They never had a home. Never had a place where they belonged instead of merely being tolerated. Eventually, after years of saving, Desire purchased a half-ruined tavern in Phandalin: [[The Sleeping Giant Tavern]]. For the first time in their life they had a place that couldn’t reject them.

---

# Personality

Desire is gentle. Soft-spoken. Patient.

They are not malicious.

They are convinced they have found a cure for loneliness, grief, and fear.

They see violence, conflict, and suspicion as sicknesses of isolated minds.

> “Everyone in this town hurts… they just pretend they don’t. I can hear it now. I can help them. I _am_ helping them.”

They do not want to fight the party.

They want to _save_ them.
