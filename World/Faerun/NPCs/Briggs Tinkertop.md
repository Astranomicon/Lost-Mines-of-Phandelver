## Background
Siv's apprentice is a gnome named **Briggs Tinkertop**, a bright-eyed and eager young craftsman who is still learning the finer points of item forging and gadget creation. Standing at just under three feet tall, Briggs sports a mop of curly brown hair that often sticks out from under his oversized leather cap. His round face is perpetually smudged with soot or grease, and his nimble fingers are always ink-stained from hours spent sketching designs or tinkering with tools far too big for him.

Though prone to mistakes, Briggs has an insatiable curiosity and an enthusiasm for the trade that’s hard to ignore. He’s constantly asking questions—sometimes more than Siv has time to answer—and tends to jump into projects headfirst, often leading to small explosions or misfires. Despite the occasional mishap, Siv sees great potential in the gnome, appreciating his knack for problem-solving and his sheer determination to improve.

When he’s not assisting Siv in the forge, Briggs is usually found scribbling furiously in his notebook, dreaming up new inventions, or practicing on smaller gadgets, hoping to one day create something truly special. He dreams of the day he can present Siv with a flawless invention of his own, though for now, he's still navigating the path of becoming a master.

Briggs’s boundless energy, paired with his cheerful personality, makes him an endearing—if occasionally exasperating—presence in the workshop. He’s always willing to lend a hand, even if that hand sometimes causes more work than it saves.
