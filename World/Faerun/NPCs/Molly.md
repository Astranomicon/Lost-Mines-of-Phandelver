# Molly

## Description
**Race:** Halfling
**Class/Role:** Child/Exposition
**Appearance:** Molly is a bright-eyed young woman in her early twenties, with a youthful exuberance that shines through her warm smile. She has curly chestnut hair that cascades down to her shoulders, often pulled back into a loose braid to keep it out of her face while she works. Her skin is sun-kissed, hinting at long days spent outdoors, and her cheeks are lightly freckled. She dresses in simple yet practical clothing—a cotton blouse and sturdy skirts—perfect for the bustling environment of the inn.

**Personality:**  
Full of energy and optimism, Molly is known for her friendly demeanor and helpful nature. She’s quick to greet patrons with a cheerful “Welcome!” and always has a kind word to share. Despite her youth, she possesses a surprising level of maturity and responsibility, often taking on various tasks around the inn with enthusiasm. Molly is eager to learn, frequently asking Bart for advice on everything from managing the inn to serving food and drink. Her curiosity extends beyond the inn, as she loves to hear stories from travelers and dreams of one day embarking on her own adventures.

**Role at the Inn:**  
As one of [[Bart]]’s most trusted employees, Molly assists with everything from serving drinks to cleaning up after meals. She moves swiftly around the inn, balancing trays laden with food and drink while weaving between tables filled with chatting patrons. When things get particularly busy, she’s known to step up, often joking with Bart that she’s the “real owner” of [[Stonehill Inn & Provisions]]. 

**Background:**  
Molly grew up in Phandalin and has always had a deep connection to the town and its people. She started working at the inn a few years ago, helping Bart as a way to support her family and contribute to the community. Her friendly personality has made her well-liked among the locals, and many view her as a little sister figure. Despite her grounded lifestyle, Molly harbors dreams of exploring the wider world, and she often listens intently to the tales of adventurers who pass through the inn, her imagination sparking with each story. 

### **Scene for Introduction:**
As the players enter Stonehill Inn after their adventures, they are greeted by the delightful aroma of freshly baked bread and roasted meat. Molly is bustling about, carrying a tray of steaming mugs, her laughter ringing through the air. Spotting the players, she sets her tray down momentarily, her face lighting up. “Welcome to the Stonehill Inn! Can I get you anything? We just made a new batch of stew that’s to die for!” 

Her energy is contagious, and as she chats with the players, they can’t help but feel a sense of warmth and community, a reminder of the simpler joys amidst their quests.
