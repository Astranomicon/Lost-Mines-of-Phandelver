Ches 5th, 1500 DR

The group stands above a hole in the ground that leads down into what looks like an old abandoned house. There are two floating pyramid shaped beings down below with a large grotesque pile of eggs.

Cithrel shoots a Guiding Bolt down at the massive slimy pile, deciding to target that first. Ralph tosses two Javelins down at the pyramid creatures. Cithrel casts a slurry of will o wisps around her before descending gracefully down the nearby vines. One approaches and her spirits tear into it, thankfully she dodges its attacks and maintains her concentration. Siv provides covering fire from above, or attempts to and then slides down into the hole nearby.

The little creature creation of Siv hits the slime mass and then responds by rolling towards them and being torn into by the will o wisps. It shoots an egg at the little creature which it tactfully dodges. Ralph joins the fray, slipping and falling into the hole and raging before bonking the egg cluster. Cithrel dodges two eggs being shot her way and also manages to dodge being attacked by a pyramid. Thankfully her will o wisps are putting work in.

Cithrel and Cricket tag team the other pyramid while Ralph focuses primarily on the big one. Unfortunately, the egg cluster slams back into him, severely hurting Ralph. Cithrel saves him from further damage. The egg cluster then explodes. One of the pyramid things succumb to the wisps and Ralph finishes off the last one. Peace at last, but Ralph is gravely injured. Cithrel tends to his wounds quickly so they may continue forth more safely. They look about and determine the house could be roughly, 100 years old perhaps? Siv stumbles upon a book from a dwarf who worships a mining god and speaks about Talhundereth. Siv and Cithrel link that Talhundereth was marked on the Goblin map and they recall from the message that stones are at each of these locations on the goblin map. They decide they must intercede these locations and prevent these stones from being brought to one another.

The group returns to Phandalin to restock, recoup and maybe pick up a horse and wagon.
